t7:stop-forked

Full name:

com.googlecode.t7mp:maven-t7-plugin:0.9.10-SNAPSHOT:stop-forked

Description:

Stops the forked Tomcat instance.

Attributes:

  • Requires a Maven project to be executed.
  • Requires dependency resolution of artifacts in scope: runtime.
